Our Story

GreenHealth Pharmacy is a community pharmacy in Punggol built by pharmacists who believe health support should be trusted, accessible, and sustainability-driven.

Our Milestones

The Beginning

GreenHealth Pharmacy was founded in 2020 by five NUS Pharmacy course-mates who came together to support the Punggol community. At the time, awareness of sustainable pharmacy practices in Singapore was still limited ‚Äî so we decided to lead by example.
