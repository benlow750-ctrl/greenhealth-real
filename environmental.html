Environmental Initiatives

We reduce waste and protect ecosystems through smarter sourcing, reuse, and safe disposal.

01

Sustainable Procurement

We work towards sourcing products and supplies from partners who prioritise responsible production and packaging practices.

  • Preference for lower-waste packaging
  • Suppliers aligned with sustainability goals
  • Smarter supply chain choices to reduce waste
02

Reusable Tote Bags

We encourage customers to bring reusable bags to reduce disposable packaging and keep plastic waste out of landfills and waterways.

  • Promotes repeat reuse instead of single-use bags
  • Supports a cleaner store-to-home journey
  • Fits with eco-friendly shopping habits
03

Refillable Sunscreen Containers

Our refill-ready sunscreen concept reduces single-use packaging by allowing customers to reuse containers instead of repurchasing new bottles each time.

  • Less plastic used per purchase over time
  • Lower packaging waste from repeat buys
  • Encourages sustainable self-care routines
04

Medication Disposal Programme

We provide a safer disposal route for expired or unused medicines so they don’t end up in household trash or water systems.

  • Reduces environmental contamination risk
  • Encourages responsible disposal behaviour
  • Supports a healthier community and ecosystem
05

E-Receipt Option

Digital receipts help reduce paper use while making it easier for customers to keep records and track purchases.

  • Less paper printing
  • Convenient record-keeping
  • Supports greener daily habits
06

Biodegradable Shopping Bags

When disposable bags are needed, biodegradable alternatives can reduce long-term waste impact compared to conventional plastic.

  • Lower persistence in the environment
  • Better alternative for unavoidable bag use
  • Supports waste reduction goals
