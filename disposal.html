SAFE MEDICINE DISPOSAL

Safely dispose of unwanted medicines

Unwanted medicines at home can be risky for children and pets. Incorrect disposal can also harm the environment. We make safe disposal simple — and responsibly handled by our pharmacy team.

Hand them to our team for safe, responsible disposal.
