Pharmacy Services

Pharmacist-led care tailored for modern families and health-conscious individuals.

01

Counselling & Advice

Our pharmacists share clear, practical guidance on medicines and supplements—so you know how to take them correctly, what to watch out for, and how to store them safely.

  • Medicine use: correct timing, dose, and technique
  • Side effects: what’s expected vs when to seek help
  • Storage: keep medicines stable and effective
02

Medicine Dispensing

Professional dispensing with safety checks—so your medicines are appropriate, accurate, and used with confidence.

  • Dispensing by trained pharmacists with verification checks
  • Dietary counselling (food–medicine considerations)
  • Supplement & complementary medicine advice
  • Minor ailment guidance (when self-care is suitable)
03

Safe Medicine Disposal

Unwanted medicines at home can be risky for children and pets. Incorrect disposal can also harm the environment. We make safe disposal simple.
